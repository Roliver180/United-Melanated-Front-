About UMF

The United Melanated Front (UMF) is a global movement dedicated to the sovereignty and unity of melanated people worldwide. We reject colonial narratives and affirm our Indigenous identity and heritage.

Our mission is to build generational wealth, protect our communities, and restore our cultural legacy through education, economic development, and collective action.

UMF stands as a beacon of hope and empowerment, creating pathways for self-determination and true liberation for melanated people across the diaspora.

Our Mission

"To unite melanated people worldwide in the pursuit of sovereignty, rejecting colonial narratives and reclaiming our Indigenous identity. We build generational legacy through education, economic empowerment, and cultural restoration."
